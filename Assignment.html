"Learning is the process of acquiring new understanding, knowledge, behaviors, skills, values, attitudes, and preferences...Learning starts at birth (it might even start before, in terms of an embryo's need for both interaction with, and freedom within its environment within the womb) and continues until death as a consequence of ongoing interactions between people and their environment."
