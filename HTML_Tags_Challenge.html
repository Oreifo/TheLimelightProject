Riding The Wind

Author: Oreifo

'Riding the wind' is a spectacular phenomenon.In a simple term, it is the act of leveraging on the strength
of the wind to accomplish a determined goal,such as soaring, like the eagle does in the midst of a daring
tempest.

About the Eagle and the Wind Unlike other birds that dart for cover in the midst of a tempest, the eagle delights in its arrival instead.
To the eagle, it is just another opportunity to relish the joy of being the king of the sky....which is usually
the dream or, better put, "the limit" of other creatures. Like every endeavour in life, however,"nothing
worthwhile comes easily." Meaning before the eagle can enjoy the joy in Riding the wind,it has to
engage the process of vigorously flapping its ususlly heavy wings to propel itself into the depths of the sky,
breaking through the tempest which, ironically, eventually equips it to glird carefreely as it soars high above the
sky.
