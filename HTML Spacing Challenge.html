Discovering Your Kitchen Tricks

I have taken the time to list below, a few steps to take in your journey to discovering the hidden cooking treasures in you
which you never knew were there.

  • Think about what special dish you want to cook.If possible,give it a name.
  • Imagine the type of feedbacks you love to receive from the consumers.
  • Make an ELABORATE list of possible ingredients needed.It should include even
    those that may seem ridiculous to you.
  • Arrange the ingredients in the various combinations you intend to try out.
  • Start cooking, making sure you note the amount/quantity of each ingredient
    used and the procedures undertaken.

  • Taste the resulting outcome. You can also make trustworthy persons with good
    taste buds do the tasting for you too.
  • Reshuffle the ingredients and cook.Do this as many times as possible until
    you produce some wonderful dish that is unique to you, which you believe is incredible.
  • Repeat the tasting procedures using the service of as many trusted tasters
    as possible and pay a close attention to their feedbacks;afterall,your end result is
    pleasing consumers who,in the end, acknowledes your Genial abilities and your Genuity
  • Make adjustments where necessary, try it out again and...HAPPY DISCOVERING!!!
